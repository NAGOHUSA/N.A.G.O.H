Privacy Architecture

Your data never
leaves your phone.

We built NAGOH with a simple principle: you shouldn't have to trust us with your data. So we engineered it so you never have to.

🔑

Privacy Protected

Your App Store Data is yours and yours alone, we store none of it.

📱

On-Device Storage Only

Sales data, app names, revenue figures — everything is stored locally using SwiftData. Nothing is transmitted to any server we operate.

🍎

Simple, Secure Setup

Add your App Bundle ID to NAGOH. NAGOH creates a unique device-specific push notification URL you add to App Store Connect. That's it.

🚫

No Analytics. No Tracking.

Zero analytics about how you use the app. No crash reporters that phone home. No behavioral tracking. Your usage is yours alone.

Setup

Up and running
in three steps.

No complex configuration. No new accounts. Just connect and go.

1

Secure Setup

Create an App profile and add the unique BUNDLE-ID address that is assigned to your app via Apple Connect. A unique and encrypted device specific URL is generated, add this to your App Connect settings for that app.

2

Smart Monitoring

NAGOH checks for new sales using Apple's official Push Notification servers. Efficient, accurate, and battery-friendly background processing.

3

Instant Alerts

The moment a sale happens, your iPhone notifies you. Track trends, celebrate milestones, stay in sync with your business.
