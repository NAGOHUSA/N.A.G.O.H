How It Works

Simple process, real results

Whether you're selling an unfinished project or buying one, the process is designed to be clear, fast, and fair for everyone involved.

For Sellers

01
Pay the Listing Fee

A small one-time fee keeps the marketplace spam-free and ensures only serious sellers list their projects.

02
Submit Your Project

Fill in your project details — title, description, platform, tech stack, price, and your seller payment link.

03
Admin Review

Our team reviews each submission to ensure quality and relevance before it goes live on the marketplace.

04
Get Discovered

Your listing is published and developers actively looking to buy browse and contact you directly to purchase.

For Buyers

01
Browse Listings

Filter by platform or tech stack and explore curated projects that match what you're looking to build.

02
Review the Details

Each listing includes a description, platform, tech stack, and a link to the code repository for due diligence.

03
Buy Directly from Seller

Click "Buy from Seller" to go to the seller's own payment page. Transactions happen directly between buyer and seller.

04
Take Ownership

Once paid, work with the seller to transfer the repository and any assets. You now own the project outright.
